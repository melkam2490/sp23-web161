About Me

Upon arriving in the USA, Melkam enrolled in ESL class and finished my ESL class. After that she
started an IT career, eager to expand her knowledge and skills in the ever-evolving world of technology. She embraced the opportunity to study in a diverse and vibrant environment, which further enriched her educational experience.

She enjoys connecting with her Ethiopian roots and maintaining a sense of identity within the multicultural tapestry of the United States. She finds inspiration in the diversity of experiences and perspectives that she encounters, fostering a deep appreciation for different cultures.

Looking toward the future, Melkam aspires to leverage her skills and knowledge gained as an IT student to make a positive impact in the industry. She envisions herself working in a dynamic and innovative environment where she can contribute to solving complex technological challenges. With her determination, adaptability, and passion for technology, Melkam is poised to thrive and excel in her chosen field.
